FAQ & eco info
Not all "eco-friendly" packaging is equal. Learn the difference before you buy.
Compostable vs biodegradable
Compostable
Breaks into non-toxic biomass within 180 days. Leaves no residue. TÜV Austria certified.
What we sell
Biodegradable
Can mean plastic that just breaks into microplastics. No standard definition. Often misleading.

Common questions
Can I compost at home?
Yes! Our mailers are TÜV Austria OK Compost Home certified, meaning they break down in a backyard compost bin — no industrial facility needed.
What's the minimum order?
No minimum! Buy as few as 10 pieces. This is the whole point of EcoPack — tingi-style access to certified eco-packaging.
How long until it breaks down?
Within 180 days in a home compost setup. Leaves zero toxic residue — only CO₂, water, and organic matter.
Are kraft bags waterproof?
They are moisture-resistant but not fully waterproof. Great for dry goods, apparel, and accessories. For wet or frozen items, use our compostable mailers instead.
